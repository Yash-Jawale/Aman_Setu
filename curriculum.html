Curriculum

Our curriculum is developed by our in house team of educationists and researchers.

Primary School

We promise to give children a childhood! Over these four years we focus on building children’s foundation for learning and on conceptual developements through our skill based learning programs.

We use SSC textbooks supplemented by other texts, workbooks and manuals that are designed and created by our very own in house curriculum developement team. Students study

  • English
  • Maths
  • Combined Science that nurtures the quality of exploration and curiosity about the world
  • History and Geography as combined social sciences
  • Secondary Languages (Hindi and Marathi)

Secondary School

Secondary school means business! Students transition into more formal learning where the focus is on fine tuning and building upon concepts learnt previously. This is also when they begin to take formal tests. In grade 8, 9 and 10 students prepare for the 10th grade board exam.
