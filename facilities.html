School Facilities

At Aman Setu, students are exposed to a range of expereinces making them more insightful and creative.

Infrastructure

Hidden between the Sukhoi Fighter jets of the IAF station on one side & the jagged hills of the western ghats on the other . Aman Setu stands in the midst of farms on the Lohegoan-Wagholi road to the east of Pune.

Out campus is a testimony to our efforts in reducing our carbon footprint. We have strucures made from recycled materials such as a classroom built from used plastic bottles. We also have trees and patches of green thoughout forming interesting pathways for children to travel via when going frm class to class.

Remedial Program

While we are a mainstream school, we are empathetic towards children with different learning needs like LD, ADHD etc.

To ensure the diversity of each class the ratio of special needs children to mainstream children is 1:10.

School Clubs

We provide students with 5 club options within school hours from which they can choose 1 each year.

Art Club

Training for the Elementary and Intermediate Art Exams

Tabla Club

Training for the State Tabla Exam

Football Club

Training for participation in interschool and state tournaments like ZP, Reliance, PDFA, etc.

Basketball Club

Training for Basketball games

Keyboard Club

Learning to play the keyboard

Competitions

In alignment with our philosophy, we encourage students only above grade 3 to participate in comepetitve competitions like the Olympiad - English, Maths, Science and GK, Spell Bee, Sci-Fests - Model Making and other interschool cultural events.
